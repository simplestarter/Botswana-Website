About LPNHS-SHS

The best senior high school in Las Piñas

Since its inception, our senior high school program has been designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and real-world problem-solving skills. We offer specialized tracks that align with the diverse interests and career aspirations of our students, empowering them to choose a path that best suits their strengths and passions.

As we look towards the future, we remain committed to our core values of academic excellence, integrity, and the holistic development of our students. We invite you to join us on this remarkable journey of learning, growth, and endless possibilities.

Experience the legacy of Las Pinas National High School, where quality education, dedicated teachers, and a vibrant community come together to shape the leaders of tomorrow. Together, let's create a brighter future.
