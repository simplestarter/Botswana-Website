Strands We Offer

Las Pinas National High School - Senior High School gladly offers you diverse range of academic program aligned to the Department of Education SHS program. Academic track, STEM and ABM strand, and TVL track, EIM. Each strand offers core and specialized subjects that equip the students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in their chosen tertiary education program. With this comprehensive approach to education, our school is committed to preparing students for the demands of their chosen career paths.

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics program provides a comprehensive educational program for primary and secondary students. The program is specifically designed to prepare students for college, graduate studies, and future careers in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

ABM

Accountancy, Business, and Management program provides student's to develop analytical and critical thinking skills, financial literacy, and understanding of business concepts and practices. The program is specifically designed to prepare students for future careers in fields such as accounting, finance, marketing, human resources, and business management.

TVL (EIM)

Technical-Vocational Livelihood (Electrical, Installation and Maintenance) track provides students with knowledge and skills needed for electrical installation, maintenance, and repair work. It aims to prepare students for future careers as electrical technicians, electrical maintenance personnel, or related roles in industries such as construction, manufacturing, telecommunications, and utilities.
