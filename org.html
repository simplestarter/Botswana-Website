Facilities

New and Modern

Our school is equipped with facilities designed to provide students with a conducive and enriching environment for their educational journey. From modern classrooms to specialized laboratories and well-maintained teachers' facilities, we are committed to ensuring that our students have access to the resources they need to thrive academically and holistically.

Computer Laboratory

The Computer Laboratory at Las Pinas National High School located at LGU Bldg. Rm 404 is a designated area within a school where students have access to computers and other technology tools for academic and learning needs. It is an essential resource for computer literacy, the development of digital skills, and the inclusion of technology in the curriculum.

Library

The Learning Resource Center (LRC/Library) at LGU Bldg. Rm 301, is a hub of knowledge and exploration. It is a vibrant space where students can engage in independent study, research, and collaborative learning. Stocked with an extensive collection of books, digital resources, and multimedia materials, the LRC provides a supportive environment for students to enhance their academic skills and pursue their intellectual interests.

Science Laboratory

The Science Laboratory at Las Piñas National High School located at LGU Bldg. Rm 202 is a designated area where students may engage in practical experiments and hands-on learning to better grasp scientific principles. It is an essential part of scientific instruction because it provides a secure, controlled setting in which students may carry out experiments, record observations, examine data, and draw conclusions.

Guidance Office

The Guidance Office at Las Piñas National High School located at is a place where students may go for advice, assistance, and counselling to take care of their academic, social, emotional, and personal needs. It acts as a gathering place where students may express their concerns, request assistance with academic preparation, look into possible career paths, and access tools for personal growth.

Faculty Office

The SHS Teacher's Faculty is a team of dedicated and highly qualified educators who are committed to providing a quality education to our students. With expertise in their respective fields, our teachers foster an atmosphere of academic excellence and personal growth.

School Clinic

The School Clinic is a recognized medical facility. It offers healthcare services and supports the health of students, staff, and faculty. During school hours, it acts as the main point of contact for dealing with minor illnesses, injuries, and health-related issues.

School-based Management Office

The School-Based Management (SBM) Office is a key administrative unit that promotes collaborative decision-making and empowers stakeholders in the educational process. The SBM Office works closely with teachers, parents, students, and community members to ensure effective governance and management of the school.

Cafeteria

The school cafeteria offers a vibrant space where students can enjoy delicious meals in a friendly atmosphere. With a diverse menu catering to various tastes and dietary preferences, our cafeteria provides nutritious options to fuel students for their academic pursuits. Whether grabbing a quick bite between classes or socializing with friends during lunchtime, the LPNHS SHS cafeteria is a hub of energy and community.
